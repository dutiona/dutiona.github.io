Hello, and welcome to my personal webpage. I am Michaël, a postdoctoral researcher at the [LRE](https://www.lre.epita.fr/) in France. My research is in theoretical computer science applied to image processing. During my Ph.D. I designed a complete _Concept_ framework for the image-processing domain, delivered through the modern C++ [Pylene library](/projects/1_project/).

I now investigate how to bridge the **static world** (C++ generic code) and the **dynamic world** (Python), where most of the image-processing community resides — exploring avenues such as JIT compilation to produce optimal Python bindings on-the-fly, on demand from the practitioner.

On a personal note, I am active on Reddit ([r/cpp](https://reddit.com/r/cpp)). I like to read good books, travel and see interesting places, good (French) food, and good music such as Pink Floyd, Rammstein, and the like.
